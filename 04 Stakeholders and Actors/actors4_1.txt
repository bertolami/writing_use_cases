Use Case: Collect Money from Vending Machine
Scope: Vending Machine
Primary Actor: Owner of the Vending Machine
Level: Summary
Stakeholders: 
 - Owner: Wants to collect the money in the vending machine
Main Success Scenario
1: Owner enters admin key
2: Vending machine accepts key and unlocks itself
3: Owner opens the machine and takes out the money
4: Owner closes machine and locks it with his admin key
